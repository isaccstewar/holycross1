About
Sub Heading

The Holy Cross Church youth fellowship has a diverse linguistic base. Like our church, the youth is a seamless unit free from the labels of language, background, status and also of age. Often we have members from the Sunday school and the men’s and women’s fellowship actively participating in our midst. The youth fellowship is a forum that provides social interaction and spiritual support while we engage ourselves in an increasingly competitive world.

Sub Heading
