Women's Fellowship

The women’s fellowship of Holy Cross Church is a unit of the Civil Area Women’s Fellowship and Karnataka Central Diocese Women’s Fellowship. It was formed under the pioneering spirit of Mrs. Devadason and Mrs. Chacko in 1977. It was at the initiative of the women’s fellowship that the pre-school was established, under the KCD women’s fellowship project, that has now become a Church supported project.
