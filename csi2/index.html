Join us in Worship

We would love for you to join us in worship if you are ever in the Jalahalli area.

You will find a warm and friendly Church with the Anglican tradition worship following the liturgy of the Church of South India.

Most of all, you will find a place where you can experience an awesome God in an awesome way!
