Sunday School

The Sunday school classroom is a space where children are taught the Bible in language that works for them so that they can carry this learning for life

At Sunday School

A typical Sunday at the Sunday school, starts at 9:30 am when kids assemble in their respective classes for a 45 minute session. Each class member has an opportunity to ask questions or express their viewpoints which provides spiritual interaction between students and their teachers and leads to a comprehensive way of learning as a group. The curriculum is formatted to meet the age specific topics and themes related to Christian faith

This is followed by 30 minutes of general devotion when all the kids from ages 2 years to 19 years gather in the Sunday school hall for a small story and a time of worship. Each week one class of the Sunday school leads in singing action songs. Kids celebrating birthdays are greeted enthusiastically. Offering is taken, and for the closing prayer we form a circle holding hands, say the Lord's Prayer together and sing the B-I-B-L-E song
