Youth Fellowship

Youth in the church community have to be nurtured spiritually to be the future pillars of the church. We epitomize the yearning for growth and progress and possess the capability to do so.

At Youth’s FELLOWSHIP

With Christ being the role model of a perfect youngster, the Youth Fellowship of CSI Holy Cross Church in all its activities and endeavors strives to share in the Lord's ministry.

When it comes to serving the church, the Youth Fellowship is in the forefront - leading in monthly praise and worship, helping regularly at Sunday School, volunteering at VBS year after year and being a part of the church decoration team. Apart from this, the youth play a very active role during the Annual Thanksgiving festival in organizing games and other fun events
