PRE SCHOOL

This mission that the church has undertaken reaches out to the community through these little ones and it is truly an opportunity to spread our Christian Love by action

At Preschool

The Pre-School program was taken up in our Church by the Holy Cross Church Women's Fellowship in 1995 as part of the project of the CSI KCD Women's Fellowship. The objective of the Pre-School program was to provide the under-privileged children in the age group of 3 to 5 years an opportunity to have the pleasure of attending a school and learning in an environment of Christian love and concern. This program was handed over to the church which currently administers it with financial support from the congregation. Every year about 25 students are admitted into the Pre-School

Mission

Children are provided with a mid-day meal every day. They are equipped with the knowledge of reading and writing the English alphabet and numerals. They enjoy physical and outdoor activities. They love to sing and dance. The children are given an opportunity to learn and explore special days like Colour Day to learn colours, Independence Day to bring in the feeling of patriotism and their own special day- their Birthdays. Every year during Christmas they highlight the reason for the season through their stage program for the congregation. They also participate in the Christmas program for the Pre-Schools organised by the KCD Women's Fellowship every year. These children are guided and supported by our efficient teachers Mrs. Navamani and Mrs. Siji Edwin.
